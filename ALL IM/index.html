The Birth of TechKaro

TechKaro officially started to take students under its wings and teach them tech-craft from the 22nd February, 2018. This program is sponsored by EngroVopak, and teaches technological and life skills to young girls from underserved communities to upskill them and provide better earning opportunities. Our Web Development course is held over a period of 5 months, and is focused on development of programming skills along with website development. Students will learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. We keep rising above and beyond every year, and to-date we have graduated a total of 160 students from our Web Development course and have placed 73 of them in jobs in internships in notable places like SastaTicket, OutSprint, BMA Capital, Savvy, CodeUp and many more.

TechKaro 2020 during the pandemic

TechKaro went completely virtual in 2020, to keep its students safe and meet the needs of the governing policies. We slowly started adjusting to the new ‘norm’ of going online, and conducted our classes on Zoom while communicating with our students on Slack.

Selection of the most fit candidate

All students have to go through a rigorous selection process that involves three distinct steps. This helps us ensure that we have selected the most deserving students who are confident and want to work in the same field. The first stage involves them studying a home study module that is sent over from our team. The second stage involves an online difficult test to assess their skills and knowledge about the module. The last step involves an interview with our instructors, this interview is to showcase their confidence and their willingness to learn

First things first- The Orientation

In 2020, Techkaro conducted its opening orientation virtually, where students were introduced to the team, the instructors and to each other. Sadaffe Abid, Founder of CIRCLE, gave a speech and boosted the morale of the new-comers while explaining the vision and mission behind TechKaro. The team from ENGRO Foundation also joined in celebrating the admission of these successful students.

TechKaro Classes for the deserving masses

The Web Development course happens twice a week and is about 4 hours long. These classes are kept interactive so that students have fun while learning, and also understand key complex topics easily.

Building on Life Skills

Life Skills are the building blocks of your professional lives. Even if you have the right skills related to technology, then you will need life skills to succeed. Some of the life skills we teach are Time Management, Resume Writing, Confidence and Resilience, Teamwork, Growth Mindset and much more. For our Coding batch of 2020, we hosted a CV-Writing life skills session in collaboration with Amal Academy, where their instructors taught our students the art behind writing a professional CV. We would also like to thank the team from Engro for constantly supporting our students and helping them strengthen their skills

Innovative Coding Projects

It is important to incorporate learnings into practise, and therefore we introduced two major projects that all students had to complete- midterm project and the final project. These two projects had a lot of weightage for students to graduate. The midterm project involved making an e-commerce website using the three front-end languages (HTML, CSS and JavaScript) that the students had learnt. Whereas, the final project was the core bit of TechKaro as students had to use Javascript library REACT to create a useful application.

International Mentor Sessions for Coding Students

TechKaro Coding students had sessions with international mentors from Thoughtswork, Klarna, Code Sandbox and more where they showcased their projects and got feedback to incorporate in their future projects. These sessions help to make the students feel optimistic about their projects, and work on any lag that can make them better.

Gearing up for the professional world

This is where we bid farewell to our promising students and set them out in the professional world. TechKaro’s team helps with the professional growth of the graduating students by hosting sessions that involve ‘interview readiness’, ‘creating a Linkedin profile’, ‘making an impact on the interviewers’, ‘adding weightage to your CV’, and many more. We also provide counselling sessions with team members to find out if the student is facing any difficulty or if they are having any problems in communication. New openings are shared with students so that they get inspired and keep applying. After the student lands a job/internship, we cover their stories for our social media and communicate with them on different occasions to see their growth in the organization
